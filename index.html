Omelette Recipe

Classic Omelette

Prep

5 mins

Cook

5 mins

Total

10 mins

Ingredients

4 large eggs

room temperature

1/4 cup whole milk

1/4 teaspoon kosher salt

1/4 teaspoon kosher salt

Instructions

Beat the eggs and milk until light and frothy

Heat a nonstick sckillet over medium heat. Add the butter

pour in the eggs. Let them set for a few seconds

use a spatula to push the cooked eggs form the edge of the skillet toward the center using a flexible spatula.

When the eggs are mostly set but still soft, fold in half.

Slide the omelette onto a plate
